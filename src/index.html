About Me

Have you ever looked at a website and wondered, why is this so hard to use? Me too! Ever since I can remember, I have been using the internet for different purposes. Whether for applying to school, searching for Wikipedia articles or nowadays watching the news. I always thought, what if I could make a better version of this? So, I learned how to code.

As a full stack developer, I build and optimize the performance and accessibility of high-impact web applications with user-friendly interfaces. I have proven this ability throughout my career with projects such as "Seneca College's International Student Application Website" which is used by thousands of students around the world.

My curiosity drives me to be a lifelong learner. I am constantly learning new skills such as Cloud Computing and Online Marketing in order to offer better insights into modern problems.
